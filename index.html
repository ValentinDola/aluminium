About Us

Who we are

Groupe Aluminium LTD. is a registered Ghananian entity in the Aluminium Industry. The Company has won and executed throughout Ghana many contracts, both in public and private, to design, fabricate and install Aluminium glazed sliding doors and windows, hinge and swing doors, protected windows, balustrades, cubicles and glass curtain walling.


What we do

We design, fabricate and install Aluminium glazed sliding doors and windows, hinge and swing doors, protected windows, balustrades, cubicles and glass curtain walling.


More

Groupe Aluminium LTD. also has four Chief Technicians who are specialist in the design, manufacture and installation of Aluminium glazed doors and windows, etc. Groupe Aluminium LTD. also gives practical training to a few selected Ghanaian youth in design, fabrication and installation from which they graduate as technicians.
